# TourPlanner

# UX

This is the wireframe for our **Tour Planner** app, designed to make creating, managing, and tracking tours simple and intuitive. The layout is clean and organized, ensuring a smooth user experience while keeping all essential features easily accessible.

## **Design & Navigation**
- The main window is divided into two key sections:
  - **Tour List (Left Panel):** Users can browse through saved tours and quickly add, modify, or delete them.
  - **Tour Details (Right Panel):** Once a tour is selected, detailed information appears alongside a map for easy route visualization.
- The **top navigation bar** includes *File* and *Options* dropdowns for importing/exporting data, configuring settings, and generating reports.

## **Core Features**
### Creating & Managing Tours
- Users can add new tours by entering details such as **start & end locations, transport type, distance, and estimated time**.
- A route image can be uploaded for visual reference.
- Tours can be easily modified or deleted with confirmation prompts to prevent mistakes.

### Tour Logs
- A built-in **logging system** allows users to track each journey by entering:
  - Date & time
  - Total distance and duration
  - Comments, ratings (1-5), and difficulty level
- Logs can be added, edited, or removed, making it easy to keep a history of trips.

### Map Integration
- The map provides a visual representation of selected tours, enhancing navigation and trip planning.

### Date & Time Picker
- Users can select dates conveniently with the calendar widget, making log entries more accurate.

## **Why This Works Well**
- **User-Friendly Layout** – Everything is where you’d expect it, reducing the learning curve.  
- **Clear & Concise Forms** – Minimal fields keep things efficient while allowing enough customization.  
- **Error Prevention** – The delete confirmation popups prevent accidental loss of data.  
- **Customizable Experience** – Users can modify tours, add logs, and adjust settings based on their needs.  

## **Final Thoughts**
We built this wireframe with usability in mind, focusing on a simple yet effective way to **plan, track, and analyze tours**. Whether for casual travel or professional trip planning, this tool is designed to be **intuitive, functional, and easy to use**.
